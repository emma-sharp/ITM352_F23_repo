Thank you for choosing our services. Your satisfaction is our priority. Please note the following shipping rates based on the subtotal of your order:

If your order subtotal is less than $400, shipping is FREE.

If your order subtotal is $400 or more but less than $1000, shipping is $50.

If your order subtotal is $1000 or more but less than $1500, shipping is $100.
